Laduma Ngxokolo

Famous South African knitwear designer

Laduma Ngxokolo is a South African textile and knitwear designer, best known for his men's knitwear range inspired by traditional Xhosa beadwork. Laduma was born in Port Elizabeth in 1986 and had his first experience with textiles and design at high school. His talent earned him a bursary during his university studies at Nelson Mandela Metropolitan. He established his brand, MAXHOSA BY LADUMA, in 2011 with the desire to explore knitwear design solutions that would be suitable for the amakrwala (Xhosa initiates) traditional dress. Laduma's designs have earned him multiple awards and he is loved both locally and interbationally. He has collaorated with many brands from Magnum to IKEA.
